Welcome to the Bubble Scrimmage League

From 8:30 to 10:30 on Tuesday and Thursday nights, fierce competition ensues between two legendary teams in a battle for BSL supremecy. The Nelson Family Bubble has the most electric baseball players west of the Mississippi. Bringing raw talent, heart, and relentless energy to the diamond. Each game is a spectacle of clutch hits, jaw-dropping catches, and intense rivalries that keep fans on the edge of their seats. Under the bright lights, every at-bat and every play carries the weight of history as these warriors leave it all on the field, determined to carve their names into BSL glory.
